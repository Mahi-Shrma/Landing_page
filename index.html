About Vriksh: Cultivating Nature’s Bounty

At Vriksh, we believe in nurturing the green world around us. Our passion lies in connecting people with the beauty and benefits of plants. Here’s what makes us unique: Ayurvedic Approach: Inspired by the ancient science of Ayurveda, we embrace holistic plant care. Just as Ayurveda promotes balance and well-being for humans, Vriksh applies similar principles to plant health. We understand that a thriving plant kingdom contributes to the overall harmony of our environment1. Diverse Plant Collection: With over 170 plant species, Vriksh offers a rich variety of green companions. From medicinal herbs to ornamental trees, each plant has a story to tell. Explore our curated selection and find the perfect addition to your home or garden. Sustainable Practices: We prioritize eco-friendly methods. Our team practices water management, soil conservation, and organic fertilization. By following the wisdom of Vriksha Ayurveda, we ensure healthy growth and productivity for every plant. Community Engagement: Vriksh isn’t just a shop; it’s a community hub. Join our workshops, gardening classes, and tree-planting initiatives. Let’s grow together! Kalp Vriksha: Ever heard of the Kalp Vriksha? It’s a legendary tree that fulfills wishes and brings happiness. At Vriksh, we encourage you to grow your own Kalp Vriksha—whether it’s the fragrant Parijat (Nyctanthes arbor-tristis) or any other cherished plant2. Visit Us: Step into our cozy antique shop, where time seems to stand still. Discover unique plant-related artifacts and immerse yourself in the world of green treasures3. Join us at Vriksh as we celebrate the magic of plants. Let’s cultivate joy, one leaf at a time!
